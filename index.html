Yerin Baek

About

Yerin Baek is a South Korean singer-songwriter who debuted in 2012 as a part of a duo called 15&. Before her debut, she won first place on a talent show, Amazing Contest Star King, by performing "I Have Nothing" by Whitney Houston.

Yerin Baek eventually left the duo and had her solo debut in 2015 with her first extended play, Frank and continues to release music as a solo artist to this day.

Discography

Yerin Baek has released many albums and extended plays that have performed highly on both Korean and international charts. Yerin Baek's albums and extended plays are as follows:
